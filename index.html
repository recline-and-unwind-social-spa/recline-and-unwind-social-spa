Spa Franchise Opportunities in Asheville

For entrepreneurs seeking a fresh and flexible path in the wellness industry, Recline & Unwind Social Spa presents an exciting avenue through its unique spa franchise opportunities. The beauty of Recline & Unwind lies in its simplicity. No complicated rituals or hours-long appointments are necessary to feel relaxed and recharged. Each service is a focused, 15-minute session that delivers real therapeutic benefits in just the right amount of time. It’s self-care that’s short but never rushed—and certainly never impersonal. Guests can pick one experience or stack a few for a customized visit that suits their mood, schedule, and needs.

Services are performed in cozy zero-gravity recliners, allowing your body to melt into a position of pure comfort. At the same time, trained massage therapists focus on key areas like the scalp, hands, shoulders, and feet. Whether a gemstone facial massage awakens your skin or a salt soak that revives tired legs, each treatment will leave you feeling better fast. There’s no robe, no undressing, and no pressure to commit to hours of spa time—just show up, recline, and let the relaxation begin.



A Social Twist on Traditional Spa Days

Recline & Unwind Social Spa isn’t about isolation and silence. It’s about connection—connection to your body, your friends, and your sense of joy. The open, one-room layout invites guests to talk, text, laugh, and even post to their favorite platforms while receiving services. In this space, wellness is celebrated openly and without judgment. The atmosphere is casual and vibrant, designed for solo visits and group gatherings.

Whether it’s a mother-daughter outing, a date night, or a birthday spa party, Recline & Unwind encourages togetherness. You can sip on local wine, bubbly, tea, or soda as you enjoy your session. The spa's beverage offerings add a celebratory touch that makes each visit feel like a treat—without the usual stress or formality of high-end spa culture. The result is an experience that’s just as nourishing for your mood as it is for your muscles.



Created With Intention and Purpose

The spa’s founders, Heidi and Kelly, built Recline & Unwind with purpose and passion. Their goal was to strip away the intimidating elements of traditional spas and focus instead on what truly helps people feel better: consistent care, human touch, and a welcoming environment. They knew from experience that most people don’t need hours of massage—they need the right kind of massage in the right place at the right time.

Backed by science and inspired by compassion, their approach delivers meaningful results through brief but targeted services. With options to add natural muscle balms, exfoliating scrubs, sheet masks, and more, guests can amplify their experience without pressure. The spa also offers gentle, age-appropriate services for kids, creating opportunities for wellness to become a family practice. This thoughtfulness has helped the brand grow and attract guests of all ages and backgrounds.
